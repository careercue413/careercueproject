Creative Questions [1]

The first part of the Questionnaire

This part consists of questions asking you about your imagination and creativeness

  • Question 1

    If you were to choose a position in football, which position would you want to be?

  • Question 2

    What superpower would you want to have?

  • Question 3

    What animal would you want to be?

  • Question 4

    If a movie were made about you, what would its title be?

  • Question 5

    If someone were to think of the type of person you would be, what would you want them to think about you?

  • Question 6

    You start a new world in a game, what would you do first?

  • Question 7

    If you were able to visit a timeline of your choice, where would you go?

  • Question 8

    If there were 3 doors in front of you and only 1 door had 1 million dollars behind it, which door would you open?

  • Question 9

    You are given the ability to turn a concept into a reality, what would you pick?

  • Question 10

    If you could live in one season forever, which would it be?
