Real life situations [3]


The last part of the Questionnaire

This part consists of questions asking you about how well and how you treat real life situations

  • Question 1

    If you were anonymously transferred 1 million dollars into your bank account, what would you do with that money?

  • Question 2

    You arrive at a restaurant and have 30 minutes to finish your lunch, but the restaurant is full. What would you do in this situation?

  • Question 3

    If your friend did something really awful to you, what would you do?

  • Question 4

    If you have an assignment due in 3 days, when would you start working on it?

  • Question 5

    If your friends ask you to hang out, what would your answer be?

  • Question 6

    Tonight you have a football competition at the same time as an important family dinner. What would you do?

  • Question 7

    You are sharing a pizza with your friends and there’s one slice left, but you’re still hungry. What would you do?

  • Question 8

    You see an old lady struggling to carry a bag down the stairs, but you’re in a hurry. What would you do?

  • Question 9

    You have a presentation but need a leader. What would you do to find one?

  • Question 10

    You have an essay and need to choose a topic. Which topic would you choose?
